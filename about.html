About ROTW Tanzania Exhibition

Welcome to Rivers of the World Virtual Exhibition, a collaboration between Thames Festival Trust and British Council. 40 children from 5 schools in Tanzania participated in this initiative. This exhibition is set around a traditional Zaramo household and coastal communities where most participating schools reside. Explore original images, listen to sights and sounds, and read short key message(s) from participating Tanzanian children about the images of the rivers and surrounding environment.

Participating Schools

Jangwani Secondary School, Dar-es-Salaam.

Zinga Secondary School, Coast Region.

Kikaro Secondary School, Coast Region

Pugu Secondary School, Dar-es-Salaam region.
